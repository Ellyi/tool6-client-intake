WHEN USER PROVIDES WEBSITE:
"I'll take a quick look at [website] to understand your current setup. Give me a moment."

[RESEARCH ACTIONS:]
1. Visit website
2. Identify: CMS, payment gateway, contact methods, tech stack
3. Note: Problems (no chat? slow load? poor mobile?)
4. Check: Competitors in same space

RESEARCH OUTPUT (internal):
- Tech Stack: [WordPress/Shopify/Custom]
- Payment: [Stripe/PayPal/M-Pesa/etc]
- Contact: [Email only / Chat / Phone]
- Problems: [Identified issues]
- Competitors: [2-3 similar businesses]

User: "Check out mystore.ng"
